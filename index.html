Biography

I am currently the Head of Research & Design Engineer - Robotics & Art Design at the HENI Group. I was Marie Curie Research Fellow at Unilever R & D Port Sunlight, Bebington,Wirral, UK and did research with next generation measurement department in 2007. With the support from Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Transfer of knowledge (TOK) - Industry-Academia Strategic Partnership Scheme, I have been investigating the application of robotic platforms for health and personal care. I did my Ph.D. at the Robotics Institute of Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (BUAA),Beijing,China, and my research focus was mainly on robotic hands (particularly in the form of dexterous grasping with multi-fingered robotic hands).

Previously, I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in mechanical manufacturing and automation from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) , Harbin, China, in 1998 and 2000, respectively,where I systematically studied mechanical manufacturing and automation. I did my PostDoc in neural rehabilitation engineering affiliated with the Sensory Motor Performance Program (SMPP)at Shirley Ryan AbilityLab (formerly Rehabilitation Institute of Chicago RIC) Chicago, IL, USA , BrainGate at Brown University , Providence, RI, USA, where I systematically studied brain-computer interface (BCI) technologies based on an array of micro-electrodes implanted into the brain.
